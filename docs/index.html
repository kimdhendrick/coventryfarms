About our farm

Coventry Farms is a premium equestrian facility located only 45 minutes from the world class Colorado Horse Park. For over 25 years, Coventry Farms has been committed to the development of Littletonâ€™s best hunter/jumper riders. We provide all of our horses and riders with the best full care and training in a serene environment of excellence. Coventry Farms offers lessons for beginners all the way up through the Grand Prix level, on seasoned bomb-proof horses. Expert hunter/jumper training is available for both human and equine athletes wishing to compete at the local and national level. Clients benefit from the McConnell's decades of experience and an environment that is conducive to education and performance at the top levels of hunter/jumper competition.
